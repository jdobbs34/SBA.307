## About
Website is about food and cooking made of 3 pages. The home page talks about why creator enjoys cooking then presents some funny gifs and pictures. Then he speak about the purpose of his page which is to create a community of people sharing their recipes.


In the nav bar there is a dropdown in recipes to pick a category for you recipe to be in. Examples would be desserts, main courses, and appetizers. The Contact page is where you sign up to make a post and would be made up of 2 forms. One would be for personal info such as Name and email. The other would be for recipe name, ingredients, and photos you would like to add to post.
